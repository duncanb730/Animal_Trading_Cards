Califonia Sea Lion

A sea lion, regardless of the name, is actually more closely related to dogs than cats.

  • Scientific Name: Z. californianus
  • Average Length: Males: 2.4m, Females: 1.8m
  • Average Lifespan: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Rocky areas of the North American coastline

Females and juveniles usually have a tawny brown coloration, while males tend to be slightly darker in color. Most noticable features include a streamline shape, dog-like head, and small ears. Unlike seals, only sea lions possess visible ears.
